#  CSE Learning Roadmaps Platform

##  Problem Statement
Many engineering students struggle to find a **clear and structured learning path** in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). Due to the lack of proper guidance and scattered resources, students often feel confused about where to start and how to progress in their chosen field. Our research indicates that nearly **97% of students** face this issue at some point in their learning journey.

---

##  Proposed Solution
We propose a **web-based platform** that provides **complete learning roadmaps** for various CSE specialization fields. Each roadmap guides students **from beginner to advanced level**, helping them systematically build skills and confidence.

Unlike existing resources, this platform consolidates **all roadmaps in one place**, eliminating the need for students to search across multiple websites. Additionally, beginners can explore an overview of different CSE domains, enabling them to identify and pursue the field that best matches their interests.

---

##  Key Features
- Centralized learning roadmaps for multiple CSE domains  
- Beginner-friendly introductions to all major CSE fields  
- Structured step-by-step learning paths  
- Easy-to-use and lightweight web interface
